We are independent entity outside in the formal political and government structures. Our journey

Established in 2015

With the initial name, Teknokrat Muda Institut Teknologi Sepuluh Nopember (TMI). Started with the name of campus ITS, considering the founder and object of the initial activities were ITS students

March 2018

Changed its name to Teknokrat Muda Indonesia (TMI) and expanded its activities in the region of Surabaya City

Reformed in April 2019

Reformed, re-branding, and expanding activities at the national level with a new vision "To be the leading self-development platform for the science technology community throughout Indonesia"

Something New

Through fresh and interesting programs such as visiting conflict areas, discussing with professional figures, and conducting creative movement, TMI has benefited hundreds of people.

Ups and Down

Like the other millennial community platforms, we experience many ups and downs when doing the best for our members, programs, and the most important thing was the sustainability of our community.
